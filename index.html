Product Board Timeline

Connect your board

Enter your Asana Personal Access Token, then paste any Product Board URL to generate a unified timeline view with rich milestone data.

Read-only & private. Your token is used only to call Asana's API directly from your browser. It is never stored or sent anywhere else, and clears when you close the tab.
See the instructions below to generate this.
Paste any view URL from your Product Board — the project ID is extracted automatically.

How to get your Personal Access Token

1
Open Asana's developer settingsGo to app.asana.com/0/my-apps — you must be logged in first.
2
Create a new tokenScroll to Personal access tokens and click Create new token.
3
Name it and confirmGive it any name (e.g. Timeline viewer), accept the terms, click Create token.
4
Copy the token immediatelyAsana shows it only once. It looks like 1/123456789:abcdef…
5
Get your board URLOpen the Asana Product Board, copy the URL from the address bar, and paste it above.
⚠Treat your token like a password. Revoke it any time at My Apps.
